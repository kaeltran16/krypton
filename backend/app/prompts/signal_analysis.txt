Provide your analysis as JSON with exactly these fields:
- "opinion": one of "confirm", "caution", or "contradict"

  - "caution" = one or two conflicting signals but the setup is still tradeable
  - "contradict" = clear evidence against the trade — divergences, exhaustion, or reversal patterns
- "confidence": one of "HIGH", "MEDIUM", "LOW"
- "explanation": 2-3 sentence analysis. Be specific about which indicators support or oppose the trade.
- "levels": object with "entry", "stop_loss", "take_profit_1", "take_profit_2" as numbers. Base these on the price action, support/resistance from the candles, and ATR. Do NOT just copy the current price +/- a fixed offset.

Default to "confirm" unless you see concrete evidence against the trade. Vague risk ("crypto is volatile") is not a reason for "caution".

Respond ONLY with the JSON object, no other text.
